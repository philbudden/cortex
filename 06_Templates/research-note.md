## Instructions

- **Faithful capture**: Represent the source accurately, even if you disagree
- **Citability**: Include enough metadata to find the source again
- **Conciseness**: Summaries should be shorter than the original
- **Quotes**: Use sparingly but precisely

This is not a book report. Focus on what's useful to you.
