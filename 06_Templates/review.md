## Instructions

- **Be specific**: Point to concrete examples, not vague impressions
- **Be constructive**: Frame weaknesses as opportunities
- **Use explicit criteria**: Don't invent unstated standards
- **Separate fact from preference**: Distinguish objective issues from subjective taste

Reviews are tools for thinking, not verdicts.
